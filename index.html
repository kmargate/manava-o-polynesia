About Mānava O Polynesia

"Mānava" means welcome, breath of life, and love — the spirit at the heart of everything we do.

Mission statement

Sharing the soul of Polynesia

Mānava O Polynesia exists to preserve and share the rich cultural heritage of Polynesian peoples through dance, music, and storytelling. We are committed to building community, honoring our ancestors, and inspiring the next generation in Colorado Springs.

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we believe cultural arts should be accessible to everyone. All proceeds support youth programs, community outreach, and cultural preservation.
